The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower in all it's glory.

Here is some information about the Eiffel Tower:

  • The Eiffel Tower is on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France.
  • Construction started on January 28, 1887 and it was completed on March 31, 1889.
  • It is named after Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.
  • The tower is 330 metres tall. Its base is square, measuring 125 metres on each side.
  • Visitors can eat in restaurants or visit the observation deck that is 276 metres above the ground.
